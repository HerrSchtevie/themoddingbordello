## Elysium Estate

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/82ff8ffa-ade1-44a2-88bd-238ab4f06f9d" alt="image" />

**Location:** Southwest of Whiterun  
**Acquisition:** Find the key hidden in the garden shrine to the right of the front door  
**Vibe:** Lush, lore-friendly large farmstead

**Special Features:**
- Linked and labeled storage
- Toggleable feature books
- Day/night responsive lights
- Fully functional farm and guest house
- Immersive kitchen/dining and bath systems
- Housecarl/follower/children rooms
- Display rooms and study
- Full crafting room

**Pros:**
- Feels like a true Dragonborn retirement home
- Highly customizable
- Rare vampire/werewolf zone

**Modpage:** https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/4119

## Aemers Refuge

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7d67b4a8-804c-4368-a344-1e6aadb8cf27" alt="image" />

**Location:** South of Ivarstead, mountaintop near Rift Imperial Camp  
**Acquisition:** Explore via portal or Arcwind Point  
**Vibe:** Lore-friendly Dwemer sanctuary

**Special Features:**
- Portal hub to major locations
- Summonable teleport NPC
- Sword recharge display
- Massive crafting space
- Multi-dragon outdoor encounters
- Dwemer elevator and Blackreach bridge

**Pros:**
- Best teleport system in the list
- Strong auto-sorting and utility features

**Modpage:** https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/4751
